# TravelPin 📍

**Innovative iOS Travel Assistant: From Footprints to Memoirs.**

TravelPin is a native iOS application designed for the modern explorer. It blends precise itinerary tracking with rich media archival and AI-powered storytelling, transforming your journeys into high-end, shareable memoirs.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🛤️ Smart Footprint Mapping
- **Route Optimization**: Visualizes your daily journeys with dynamic lines connecting your spots.
- **Interactive Offline Maps**: Full interactive Map exploration using custom tile caching (OpenStreetMap) for any trip destination. No network, no problem.

### 📸 Rich Media Archival
- **Multi-Photo Selection**: Support for multiple photos per spot using native `PhotosPicker`.
- **Media Statistics**: A comprehensive review of your footprint, showcasing photo distribution and trip highlights.

### ✍️ AI-Powered Memoirs
- **Literary Assistant**: A "文學博主" (Poetic Blogger) AI persona that generates beautiful, sensory-rich travel journals from your itinerary data.
- **Editorial Layout**: Premium journal templates designed for high-end digital archiving and social sharing.

### 🧳 Trip Management
- **Packing Matrix**: A category-based luggage checklist integrated into every trip.
- **Itinerary Timeline**: A fluid, timeline-based view of your daily highlights and plans.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Core**: SwiftUI (iOS 17+)
- **Persistence**: SwiftData (Native local storage with external media support)
- **Mapping**: MapKit with custom `MKTileOverlay` for offline interactive capabilities.
- **Design**: "Liquid Glass" aesthetic (Glassmorphism, high-contrast typography).

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/chao-ace/TravelPin.git`
2. Open `TravelPin.xcodeproj` (or the folder) in Xcode 15+.
3. Ensure you have iOS 17+ targeted.
4. Run on a simulator or a physical device.

---

## 📖 Design Philosophy

TravelPin follows an **editorial-first** philosophy. We believe travel records shouldn't just be data points; they should feel like a premium digital magazine. The UI prioritizes high-quality visuals, smooth transitions (Liquid Glass), and an immersive, distraction-free environment.
